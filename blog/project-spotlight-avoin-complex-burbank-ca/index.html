    Project Spotlight: Avoin Complex in Burbank CA Featuring 6’’ Siding Profile

    Resysta USA

    51

    Project Details:

    • Avoin Complex in Burbank, CA
    • Design: Modern Commercial
    • Product: 6” Siding Profile
    • Color: Golden Pale

    The Avoin Complex in Burbank, California, is a standout example of how modern commercial design can incorporate warm, natural-looking materials without sacrificing durability or sustainability. Featuring Resysta Building Products’ 6” Siding Profile, the structure blends sleek architectural lines with the inviting tone of wood, reimagined in a high-performance composite material.

    Modern Commercial Design

    The complex showcases a clean, contemporary exterior defined by its bold contrasts, crisp white walls, expansive black-framed windows, and vertical panels in the shade of golden pale. This composition creates a striking facade that feels both professional and welcoming, ideal for a multi-use commercial space.

    The horizontal alignment of the siding elevates the building’s profile and adds a sense of scale while also emphasizing clean lines and architectural symmetry. The subtle texture of the siding adds warmth to the structure’s geometric elements, achieving a balance between modern form and material richness.

    Color Selection: Golden Pale

    Golden Pale is a bright, natural tone that adds warmth and sophistication to commercial spaces. It was selected for its ability to:

    • Enhance visual contrast between sleek modern materials like glass and steel
    • Brighten exterior elevations with a golden, sun-washed hue that feels organic and inviting
    • Maintain its color even under intense California sun, thanks to UV resistance built into the Resysta siding formula

    Whether viewed up close or from the street, this shade delivers a welcoming first impression with the enduring beauty of natural wood without the maintenance concerns.

    The Architecture: Performance Meets Visual Appeal

    The choice of Resysta Building Products’ 6” Siding Profile ensures long-term performance under demanding environmental conditions. It is highly resistant to:

    • UV radiation and fading
    • Cracking, warping, and swelling
    • Moisture intrusion and rot

    This makes it an ideal solution for commercial properties where aesthetics, durability, and low maintenance are all essential.

    The Avoin Complex in Burbank is a compelling case of commercial design that values both aesthetic impact and long-term sustainability. With its clean lines, warm tones, and advanced material selection, it reflects the future of commercial architecture where performance and visual appeal go hand in hand.

    Resysta Building Products composite siding is made from a unique blend of rice husks, common salt, and mineral oil, making it a wood-free and eco-conscious alternative to traditional cladding. The Avoin Complex exemplifies how sustainability can be seamlessly integrated into modern architecture, not just in principle but in form and finish.

    By using Resysta Building Products, the project avoids the use of tropical hardwoods and helps reduce waste, all while achieving a sophisticated, professional look that lasts. Request your FREE sample today!
